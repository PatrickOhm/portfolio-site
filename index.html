CRAZY SNAKE

Crazy Snake is a slightly modified version of the well-known snake game. The goal is to navigate the snake and collect as much food as possible. Each time the snake eats, it gets a bit longer and the difficulty level increases. In my version the entire playground rotates. For this reason it is extremely difficult to navigate the snake correctly to the next food spot. Try it out!

BLUE CAVE

Blue Cave is a simple platformer game with a very minimalistic game principle and design, coded with plain JavaScript. The goal is to master all ten levels. Therefore you have to jump on the white platforms without touching the red lava blocks. If you fail and and get burned from the lava, you start from the beginning an try again. While the first levels are easy as pie, the difficulty level increases fast. Patience and skill are required. Can you complete all ten levels?

THE MEMORY GAME

This is just my version of the well known memory game. You have to click on a card to see the picture, try to remember the position and then find the matching card. If you found two identical cards, they disappear from the display and you get a get a point. In total there are 12 cards, you have to get 6 points to bring the game to the end. Its a simple game to challenge yourself. Play again and beat your record time and your total clicks.
